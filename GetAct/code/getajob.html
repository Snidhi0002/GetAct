What do you mean by W-2 status?
+

The U.S. Department of Labor guarantees protections for employees, not freelancers (aka 1099s).

These protections include:

  • Overtime pay
  • Guaranteed minimum wage
  • Protection from unlawful discrimination and retaliation
  • Withheld state and federal income and payroll taxes from wages
  • Employer-paid Social Security, Medicare and unemployment taxes
  • Workplace safety laws
  • Workers’ compensation
  • State unemployment benefits
  • Family or medical leave 
  • Option for healthcare benefits
  • Paid sick leave
